About Us


Welcome to Sweet Slice Bakery! We are passionate about creating mouthwatering cakes for every occasion. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, anniversary, or just a craving for something sweet, we've got you covered.


Founder of Sweet Slice Bakery


Sweet Slice Bakery was founded by a trio of students known as Arkyle, which stands for April Ann Hernandez, Jake Guruela, and Karylle Bautista. Together, we're a group of passionate bakers determined to bring tasty treats to our customers.
